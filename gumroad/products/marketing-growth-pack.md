**1. jp-seo-writer -- Japanese SEO Article Generator**
AI-powered Japanese SEO content creation. Co-occurrence keyword analysis, E-E-A-T compliance, proper heading structure, and meta tag optimization. Generates articles that rank in Japanese Google, not just articles that happen to be in Japanese.

**2. jp-humanizer -- AI Text Naturalizer for Japanese**
Transforms AI-generated Japanese into natural, human-sounding text. 500+ transformation patterns across 4 modes (casual, business, formal, academic). Eliminates the "AI-wrote-this" tells that Japanese readers spot instantly.

**3. google-ads-agent -- Google Ads Campaign Management**
Full Google Ads API integration. Campaign creation, keyword bidding, ad copy management, budget optimization, and performance reporting. Handles Japanese keyword matching and ad approval requirements.

**4. ga4-search-console -- GA4 + Search Console Unified Analytics**
Unified analytics across GA4 and Google Search Console. Pageviews, sessions, conversions, search rankings, click-through rates, and keyword performance -- all in one place. Week-over-week comparisons with JPY revenue tracking.

**5. google-maps-biz -- Google Business Profile & MEO**
Google Business Profile API integration for MEO (Map Engine Optimization). Review management, post creation, Q&A handling, and local SEO optimization for physical store businesses in Japan.

**6. social-media-publisher -- Multi-Platform Social Media Posting**
Simultaneous posting to X/Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and LINE Official Account. Content calendar management, optimal posting time scheduling (JST), and cross-platform performance tracking.

**7. sansan-agent -- Sansan Business Card Management**
Sansan API integration for Japan's leading business card management platform. Contact data extraction, lead management, CRM enrichment, and networking insights from business card data.

**8. hubspot-japan -- HubSpot CRM for Japan**
HubSpot API integration with Japanese market adaptations. Lead nurturing workflows, sales pipeline management, contact management, and marketing automation with Japanese language support.
